------------------------------

   - Kliknij węzeł
   - Settings

   - Max Tries: 2
   - Wait Between Tries: 1000 ms

C) Get Approved Summary:
   - Kliknij węzeł
   - Settings
   - Retry On Fail: ON
   - Max Tries: 3
   - Wait Between Tries: 2000 ms
